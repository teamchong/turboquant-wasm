turboquant-wasm

WASM SIMD vector compression — 3 bits/dim with fast dot product

TurboQuant compresses high-dimensional float32 vectors to ~3 bits per dimension with near-zero distortion. Inner products can be computed directly on compressed data via WASM relaxed SIMD — no decompression needed.
